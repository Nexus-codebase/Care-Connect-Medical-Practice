How your test results are reviewed

Find guidance on how test results are reviewed and when you should expect an update from the GP practice.

Review process

  • 1. Samples are processed by partner laboratories.
  • 2. A GP reviews and signs off your result.
  • 3. We contact you if follow-up is required.

Need support?

If your symptoms worsen while waiting, contact the practice for clinical advice.
