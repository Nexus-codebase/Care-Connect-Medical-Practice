About

A GP practice focused on accessible, consistent care

CareConnect GP Practice supports patients with everyday medical care, preventive health support, and a booking process designed to be simple and reliable.

Our Approach

Friendly local care with modern access

We combine compassionate GP support with simple online access so patients can book, confirm, and manage appointments with less friction.

Continuity of care

We aim to provide consistent support across routine appointments, reviews, and follow-up care.

Accessible appointments

Patients can book available appointments online and receive confirmation details without unnecessary delays.

Support for everyday health

From new concerns to long-term condition reviews, we focus on practical, compassionate primary care.

What to expect

Appointment Details

  • Standard appointments: 10 minutes
  • Extended consultations: 15-20 minutes (for complex concerns)
  • All bookings are held securely in line with NHS data protection standards
  • You will receive an email reminder 24 hours before your visit
  • Need to change or cancel? Use the link in your confirmation email - please give at least 2 hours' notice where possible

Useful Information

  • Appointments are for registered patients only, except for initial new-patient registrations
  • If no slots appear, all available time-slots have been taken - please check again later or call reception
  • For urgent medical concerns when we are closed, please contact NHS 111 or dial 999 in a life-threatening emergency
