Cancelling or changing an appointment

To cancel your appointment, use your booking details below or contact us directly using one of these options:

  • 1. Use your NHS account (through the NHS website (www.nhs.uk) or NHS App)
  • 2. Use the GP online system (SystmOnline)
  • 3. Phone us during opening times:
    Cromwell Medical Centre: 01992 624732
    Wormley Medical Centre: 01992 440877
  • 4. If your appointment is within the next 24 hours, call reception on Cromwell Medical Centre 01992 624732 or Wormley Medical Centre 01992 440877, or use the GP online system
