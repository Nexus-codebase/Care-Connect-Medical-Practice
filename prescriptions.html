Prescriptions

Prescription services

Order repeat prescriptions, nominate a pharmacy, and find guidance for medicine questions and reviews.

Ordering repeat prescriptions

Use one of these options to request repeat medication:

  • 1. Through your online patient account
  • 2. Hand in your repeat slip at reception
  • 3. Post your request with a stamped envelope
  • 4. Call the practice if you are elderly or housebound

Collecting your prescription

Prescriptions are usually ready in 3 working days. Please choose a nominated pharmacy for collection.

You can update your nominated pharmacy online, by speaking to the practice, or directly with a participating pharmacy.

Questions about medicines

Pharmacists can answer medicine questions, including over-the-counter treatments and common side effects.

If you need to discuss prescription changes with the practice, contact us through the support channels on our contact page.

Medication reviews

Patients on repeat prescriptions may be invited for routine medication reviews to keep treatment safe and effective.

Prescription charges

Charges may apply depending on eligibility. If you are unsure, please ask reception before collecting your medicine.

Old or unused medicines

Return old medicines to a pharmacy or the practice. Do not put medicines in household waste or flush them.
